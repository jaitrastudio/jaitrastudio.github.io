The Craft

Nothing here is incidental.

Sustainable Leather

Responsibly sourced hides, vegetable-tanned with natural extracts instead of harsh chemicals. Leather that ages beautifully and is made to outlast trends, not fill landfills.

Saddle Stitching

Two needles, one waxed thread, locked at every pass. A seam that holds even if a single stitch is cut, something machines cannot replicate.

Burnished Edges

Every edge is sanded, waxed, and polished by hand until it reads as a single, unbroken line of leather.

Marked as yours, in gold.

Every Jaitra piece can be hot-foil stamped with your initials, pressed by a heated brass die into the leather and finished in antique gold. A quiet signature that belongs to no one else.
